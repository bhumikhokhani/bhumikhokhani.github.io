Return Refund Status Resolver

Transforming static refund messaging into an automation-driven resolution system across high-volume marketplace flows.

Situation

Customers experiencing delayed refunds were encountering static status messaging, leading to repeat contacts and manual escalations.

Task

Redesign the refund visibility experience to improve automation rate while maintaining policy clarity and customer trust.

Action

• Mapped refund decision trees and failure states • Identified policy-triggered edge cases • Redesigned messaging hierarchy to reflect resolution confidence • Collaborated with operations and policy teams

Result

Improved automation resolution rates and reduced unnecessary support escalations.
